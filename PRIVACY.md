# Privacy Policy for Beauty of Fractals iOS

Beauty of Fractals iOS does not collect, store, track, or share any personal data.

The app does not require an account or sign-in.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection services.

Images exported by the user are created locally on the device and are not uploaded or shared by the app.
