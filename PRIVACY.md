# Privacy Policy for Beauty of Fractals iOS

Beauty of Fractals iOS does not collect, track, sell, or share personal data
with the developer or third parties.
The app does not require an account or sign-in.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or
third-party data collection services.

Fractal rendering and PNG export happen locally on the user's device. Images
exported by the user are not uploaded or shared by the app.

Favorite spots can optionally sync through the user's Apple iCloud account.
The Apple Watch companion can receive render progress, preview images, and zoom
information from the paired iPhone through Apple WatchConnectivity. These Apple
system services are used only for app features and are not connected to a
developer-operated server.
